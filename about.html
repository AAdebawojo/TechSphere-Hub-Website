Meet Our Founders

Ayomikun Adebawojo

Ayomikun Adebawojo is the Co-founder of TechSphere Hub and a passionate data enthusiast dedicated to shaping the next generation of data analysts. With over five years of hands-on experience, he works as a Power BI Developer, specializing in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights that drive business success.

Business Analysis Bootcamp

About the Course

The Business Analysis Bootcamp is crafted to train you to become a critical link between business needs and technical solutions. Whether you're switching from a non-tech background or upskilling, this bootcamp will transform you into a Business Analyst ready to drive operational success in industries like Finance, Healthcare, Technology, and Retail.

Over 12 weekends, you’ll master practical tools like JIRA, Confluence, Microsoft Visio, and Balsamiq while learning key methodologies such as Agile and Scrum. You'll develop a strong portfolio by working on business process modeling, requirements documentation, stakeholder analysis, and user story creation.

Bootcamp Benefits

  • Real-world case studies and business collaboration
  • Job Search & Interview Preparation
  • On-the-Job Support (3 Months)
  • Recommendation letter from TechSphere Hub
  • Certification of Completion + Virtual Internship Pathway

Topics Covered

  • Requirements Gathering: Learn how to identify business needs, elicit and document requirements, and define solutions that align with strategic objectives. Master best practices for requirement life cycle management, stakeholder collaboration, and business process modeling to drive successful project outcomes.
  • Stakeholder Management: Develop critical skills to engage, influence, and manage stakeholders throughout the business analysis process. Build strong relationships, manage expectations, and ensure stakeholder needs are integrated into business solutions through effective planning, communication, and collaboration strategies.
  • Agile Methodologies: Gain a solid foundation in Agile frameworks such as Scrum and Kanban, and learn how Business Analysts operate within Agile environments. Understand iterative development, MVP strategies, backlog management, and how to deliver continuous value through flexible, adaptive project methodologies.
  • Tools: Equip yourself with essential technical skills using industry-standard tools. Analyze data with Excel and SQL, build visual dashboards with Power BI, create process diagrams with Visio, design wireframes with Balsamiq, and manage agile projects with Jira and Confluence — making you a complete, tech-savvy Business Analyst.

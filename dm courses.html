Digital Marketing Bootcamp

About the Course

The Digital Marketing Bootcamp is designed to make you a master in the fast-evolving world of digital marketing — from search engine strategies to running powerful ad campaigns and building global brand presence. This course is perfect for career starters, entrepreneurs, and career switchers who want to thrive in the digital economy.

In 6 intensive weekends, you’ll dive into core areas like SEO, Google Ads, Email Marketing, and Social Media Management, while working with real tools such as Google Analytics, SEMrush, Mailchimp, and Hootsuite. You'll build and optimize your own campaigns, crafting a professional portfolio that will showcase your ability to drive measurable results.

Bootcamp Benefits

  • Build live marketing campaigns and measure real outcomes
  • Job Search & Interview Preparation
  • On-the-Job Support (3 Months)
  • Recommendation letter from TechSphere Hub
  • Certification of Completion + Virtual Internship Pathway

Topics Covered

  • SEO: Develop in-demand skills in search engine optimization by learning how to optimise websites, conduct keyword research, and build backlink strategies that improve online visibility and drive organic growth.
  • Google Ads: Learn how to create, manage, and optimise high-performing Google Ads campaigns. Gain practical experience in keyword bidding, ad copywriting, audience targeting, and budget management to maximise return on investment.
  • Email Marketing: Master the art of crafting impactful email campaigns using Mailchimp. Learn how to automate email sequences, segment audiences, design engaging content, and build strategies that nurture customer relationships and drive conversions.
  • Social Media Management: Become proficient in planning, scheduling, and analyzing social media campaigns using Hootsuite. Build strategic content calendars, engage audiences, and leverage analytics to improve brand presence across major social platforms.
  • Essential Digital Marketing Tools: Hands-on training with Google Analytics, SEMrush, Mailchimp, and Hootsuite — the leading tools every digital marketer must master to create data-driven campaigns, optimise performance, and deliver measurable results.
